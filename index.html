SHERIDAN TONGUE

COMPOSER FOR FILM AND TELEVISION

Sheridan is a BAFTA nominated composer who has proven success and versatility in composing tracks from full orchestration to sparse contemporary soundtracks, evident in the scores for his award winning television dramas and documentaries. His individual and original approach to composing has kept him highly in demand.
