SHERIDAN TONGUE

COMPOSER FOR FILM AND TELEVISION

Sheridan is a BAFTA nominated composer, with proven success and versatility composing tracks from full orchestration to sparse contemporary soundtracks, evident in the scores for his award winning television dramas and documentaries. His individual and original approach to composing has kept him highly in demand.

ABOUT

Sheridan’s diversity is evident in the spectrum of drama and documentary in his portfolio. Two of his most recent projects also demonstrate this - the epic Discovery Channel series INTO THE UNIVERSE WITH STEPHEN HAWKING makes use of full orchestra with bespoke sound design and electronic elements. His unique soundtrack for the hit series WONDERS OF THE SOLAR SYSTEM and WONDERS OF THE UNIVERSE featured heavily processed guitars, orchestral elements, drums & loops, solo bowed instruments and his own eclectic mix of custom samples.

Sheridan has also scored the music to award winning UK television dramas in the thriller genre including MI5 (SPOOKS UK) (BAFTA nomination for Best Original Music 2004), the popular BBC crime drama SILENT WITNESS (series 9-16) and the contemporary crime drama series DCI BANKS for Leftbank Pictures (series 1&2).

For the documentary WOUNDED (BAFTA Best Single Documentary 2009), Sheridan created a strong emotive soundtrack with a very sparse contemporary feel. He has also created a lighter tone with his score to the BBC children’s drama series SUMMERHILL and a lush feel to the Discovery Channel Series ATLAS 4D.

He has composed the music for two 13-part children’s television action/adventure series for CBBC called the SPARTICLE MYSTERY.

Born in Belfast Sheridan studied clarinet and music at the City of Belfast School of Music and then obtained an Honours degree at Surrey University in Music & Sound Recording (BMusTon, 1989). While at Surrey he won the prestigious Jacques Levy prize for best electroacoustic composition and the prize for Best Final Year Recording Portfolio. He also composed his first film score during his final year for an Angela Carter short film called The Kitchen Child.

On graduating he worked in many studios as a keyboard player, programmer, remixer and orchestral arranger working with artists such as; Beverly Knight, Jocelyn Brown, The Verve, Blur, Robert Plant and Joey Negro. From Sheridan’s time working in the pop field with many successful artists, he built up a large repertoire of world class musicians that he regularly draws upon to play on his compositions.

Sheridan has wide experience working in advertising, having written music to accompany global campaigns for a number of major clients including NISSAN, COCA COLA, and LG.

Sheridan has studios in the USA and UK and is currently working on series 3 of DCI BANKS for Leftbank Pictures and Series 17 of Silent Witness for the BBC.
